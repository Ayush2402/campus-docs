Why choose BMSCE CAMPUS DOC?

Advanced digitization and secure document management for academic excellence

Secure Role-Based Access

Advanced security with three distinct roles: Super User, Admin, and User with precise access control for document management.

Digital Document Upload & Scan

Digitize documents through direct upload or scanning with automatic UPPERCASE filename validation for consistency.

Secure Cloud Storage

Digitized documents stored securely in the cloud with organized structure: Year → Branch → Category for efficient management.

Instant Document Retrieval

Quickly access digitized documents by year, branch, and category with advanced search and intuitive navigation.

User Management

Super Users can add new users, assign roles, and manage permissions across the entire system.

Mobile Responsive

Access documents and manage uploads from any device with our mobile-optimized interface.
